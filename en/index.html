About

Putting Credibility First

Cao Minh Luat Law Office was established on March 19, 2010 under registration certificate No. 41.01.1227/TP/ĐKHĐ issued by the Ho Chi Minh City Department of Justice, and operates under the Ho Chi Minh City Bar Association.

With credibility as our top priority, we carry out everything we commit to our clients in the smartest and most dedicated way.

Cao Minh Luat brings together experienced and dynamic lawyers, each with years of expertise in their respective fields. Most of our members and associates are fluent in both Vietnamese and English. We provide high-quality legal services at reasonable cost to individuals, organizations, and domestic and international businesses operating in Vietnam.

Legal Services

Practice Areas

We provide a broad range of legal services for individuals, domestic businesses, and international enterprises.

Corporate Advisory

Comprehensive legal support for domestic businesses and FDI companies operating in Vietnam.

  • Business-specific contract advisory
  • Advisory for capital contributors and shareholders
  • Advisory on M&A and transfer of capital contributions
  • Advisory on charter amendments, capital increases or decreases, and conversion of entity type
  • Advisory on bankruptcy and dissolution procedures

Civil and Land Advisory

Comprehensive advisory on civil-law and land-law matters for individuals and organizations.

  • Advisory on sale, transfer, and gift contracts relating to land and real estate
  • Drafting and review of civil contracts such as loans, leases, services, and transportation agreements
  • Advisory on guarantees, pledges, mortgages, and deposits
  • Advisory on labor contracts
  • Advisory on administrative procedures

Investment and Licensing

Supporting domestic and foreign investors through every stage of investment in Vietnam.

  • Company establishment for foreign investors
  • Advisory on procedures for investment licenses and other sub-licenses
  • Advisory on periodic reporting obligations for foreign-invested companies
  • Drafting and review of commercial contracts and joint venture agreements
  • Advisory on trademark registration, product circulation registration, and related matters

Inheritance and Family Matters

Advisory on inheritance, wills, marriage, and family matters, especially cases with foreign elements.

  • Advisory on inheritance declaration and estate distribution procedures
  • Advisory on inheritance dispute resolution
  • Drafting and review of wills and estate distribution plans
  • Advisory on divorce, child custody, support, and property division involving foreign elements

Tax and Accounting

Advisory on tax law and accounting management for businesses.

  • Tax-law advisory and tax cost optimization
  • Advisory on compliant accounting management
  • Advisory on tax dispute resolution

Import and Export

Comprehensive legal advisory for import-export operations.

  • Drafting and review of import-export contracts
  • Advisory on international payment methods such as L/C, T/T, and D/P
  • Advisory on international trade dispute resolution

Commercial Mediation

Negotiation, settlement, and mediation support for disputing parties.

  • Participation in negotiation and settlement discussions on behalf of clients
  • Advisory on practical and cost-efficient mediation options
  • Mediation support and drafting of settlement minutes for commercial and civil disputes

Court Litigation

Representation and protection of client interests.

  • Representation in negotiations and litigation before courts at all levels
  • Participation in commercial arbitration proceedings
  • Preparation of lawsuits, case files, and evidence
  • Advisory on effective dispute-resolution strategies
